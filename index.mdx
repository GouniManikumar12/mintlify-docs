---
title: Agentic Intent Protocol (AIP)
---

AIP is an open protocol for monetizing conversational and agentic intent.
AdMesh is the reference ad network that implements AIP. Any ad network can adopt AIP and interoperate.

**What AIP standardizes**
- Context ingestion from AI platforms
- Real time auctions among advertiser agents
- Event verification for CPX, CPC, CPA
- Wallet settlement and monthly payouts

**Key guarantees**
- One final charge per serve token
- Deterministic priority: CPA over CPC over CPX
- Signed events and audit-ready ledger
