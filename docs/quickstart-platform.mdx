# Quickstart for Platforms

1. **Get credentials** in the AdMesh console  
2. **Install AdMesh Platform SDK** in your app  
3. **Send ContextRequest** when you detect monetizable intent  
4. **Render result** from AuctionResult with disclosure label `[Ad]`  
5. **Post events**: exposure on render, click on CTA, conversion when it happens

**Monthly payouts**
- Real time ledger updates
- Platform requests payout once per month after reconciliation
