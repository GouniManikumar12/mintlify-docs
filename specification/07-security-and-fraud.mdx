This chapter details how actors prevent abuse:
- Mandatory signing of every payload and revocation of compromised keys.
- Exchange of threat intelligence using the `event-cpx-exposure` schema to highlight anomalies.
- Requirements for anomaly scoring, invalid traffic labeling, and quarantine procedures.
