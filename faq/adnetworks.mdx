<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the Ad Network's role in AIP?">
    The Ad Network mediates auctions, verifies events, manages wallets, and maintains the audit ledger across all participants.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are auctions scored?">
    Auctions use a weighted scoring formula balancing price, relevance, and trust scores. The highest total score determines the AuctionResult.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is fraud prevented?">
    Each message includes a cryptographic signature and nonce. Duplicate or tampered requests are rejected automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are payouts reconciled?">
    Verified ledger entries are aggregated by wallet each month. Ad Networks perform reconciliation before triggering payouts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens during a dispute?">
    Disputed events are reviewed against ledger hashes. If invalid, funds revert to the originating wallet and an updated revision_id is recorded.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
