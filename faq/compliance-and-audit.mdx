title: "FAQ – Compliance and Audit"
description: "Frequently asked questions about AIP compliance, privacy, and audit trails"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does AIP meet privacy regulations?">
    AIP complies with GDPR, CCPA, and similar frameworks through anonymization, consent management, and minimal data retention.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are audit trails stored?">
    All verifications, signatures, and timestamps are written to the Ad Network’s immutable audit ledger.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the data retention policy?">
    Event logs: 90 days. Ledger entries: 5 years. Wallet records: 7 years. Aggregated metrics: anonymized and retained indefinitely.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are cryptographic keys managed?">
    Each participant uses rotating key pairs. Keys are renewed every 90 days, with backward compatibility for verification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are breaches handled?">
    If a participant is compromised, ledgers lock automatically and key rotation occurs. Impacted participants are notified for re-verification.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
