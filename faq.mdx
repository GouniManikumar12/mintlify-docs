# AIP FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about AIP adoption, interoperability, payouts, and the role of AdMesh as the reference operator.

## Is AIP only for AdMesh?
No. AdMesh is the reference operator. Any ad network can implement AIP.

## Do data providers get payouts?
No by default. Only platform and ad network in v1.0.

## Why monthly payouts?
Reduces operational overhead and gives time to reconcile disputes.
