---
title: Events
---

**Exposure (CPX)**
- Fired on render
- Includes surface, position, visibility

**Click (CPC)**
- Fired on CTA
- S2S or client

**Conversion (CPA)**
- Fired on verified action
- For agentic sessions, “conversion” can be task completion

**Windows**
- CPX → CPC: 30 min
- CPC → CPA: 24 h
- CPA fallback: 7 days
