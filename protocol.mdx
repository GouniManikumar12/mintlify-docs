---
title: Protocol basics
---

**Transport**
- HTTPS or gRPC
- HMAC signature over method, path, canonical body, timestamp, nonce
- Header `X-AIP-Version: 0.1`

**Identifiers**
- platform_id, agent_id, wallet_id, session_id, request_id, auction_id, bid_id, serve_token

**Timestamps**
- RFC 3339 UTC
